# FAQ

## Do I need a real company environment?
No. A fictional environment is recommended for safety and shareability.

## How many assets and risks should I include?
- Assets: 10–15
- Risks: 5–8

## Does the mapping have to be perfect?
No. It needs to be logical and defensible.

## Can I use both ISO and NIST?
Pick one for v1. You can add cross-mapping later.

## What makes this “portfolio-grade”?
Clear environment context, plain-English risks, control mapping, and evidence thinking.

# FAQ

## Do I need a real environment?
No. A fictional environment is recommended.

## How many assets/risks?
Assets: 10–15  
Risks: 5–8

## Does mapping need to be perfect?
No. Logical and defensible is the target.

## Can I do ISO and NIST?
Not for v1. Pick one. Cross-mapping is a later upgrade.

## What makes this stand out to hiring managers?
Evidence planning + risk decisions + clear scoping.
